Mentorship that helps students see themselves in physics, leadership, and the future of STEM.

Why MCP

Elegance, access, and belonging should coexist in every scientific pipeline.

Mission

MCP provides one-to-one mentoring, professional visibility, and long-horizon support for students finding their way in physics and related STEM careers.

Story

The program began with a simple, urgent question: what becomes possible when students can finally see themselves in the field they love?

Impact & Stewardship

A small program with a compounding effect on belonging, leadership, and professional access.

Momentum

William & Mary coverage published on March 24, 2026 reported that MCP had already supported more than 120 students through mentoring, visibility, and career-facing community.

Network Effect

MCP brings students into conversation with scientists, engineers, executives, and alumnae whose examples widen what students believe is possible after physics.

Investment Lens

Support for MCP translates into visible student confidence, enduring professional relationships, and a program story worthy of institutional pride and philanthropic trust.

Program Model

Built for career imagination, professional access, and trust.

One-to-One

Personal mentorship with real professional proximity

Students are connected with mentors who model many different futures in physics, engineering, computing, research, policy, and leadership.

Career Fluency

From coursework to confidence to opportunity

MCP translates scientific training into practical career pathways by opening conversations about roles, workplaces, and choices that students may not otherwise encounter.

Intergenerational

A network that compounds over time

Alumnae, professionals, faculty, and student leaders all shape the culture. The result is not a one-off program, but a strengthening ecosystem.
