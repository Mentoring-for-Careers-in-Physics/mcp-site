Why Give

Support that reaches students directly.

MCP creates the kind of support students remember for years: a mentor who can answer honest questions, a conversation that changes someone’s confidence, an event that makes a future path feel real.

Support helps keep the program welcoming, practical, and sustainable as it continues to grow at William & Mary.
